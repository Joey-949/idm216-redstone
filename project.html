Team Aperiogon's
Redstone Pizza Food Truck App

Updated Maps and Personas

The affinity map for Redstone Pizza is about what the major goals are that we need, which is efficiency. Being quick with the order should be our number 1 priority, with our persona representing that, having a person who has small lunch breaks, and has to go from one place to another in a short amount of time, while also getting food.

Updated Styles and Boards

The Mood boards are an easy example on what should be the design goal of Redstone Pizza, with deep oranges and a marinara sauce like color, while also having a solid color like white to balance it out. Which is prominent in our style sheet. Choosing this style of color and font that resonates with the branding of Redstone Pizza.

Critical Path Basic Wireframes

1.The Logo shows up for a few seconds when opening the app 2.Main menu appears, user clicks on "start a new order" 3. User gets a time to order pizza 4.User chooses custom pizza 5.User picks a type of base 6.User selects toppings on the pizza 7.User is shown the menu again but with a cart button on the bottom 8.User clicks on cart to see their order, and clicks the payment button 9. User reviews order, and confirms it 10. User is guided to a time limit on when the pizza is done, along with the location of the business.
